Origin Story

H-Thai-ML was founded in 1952 in Durham, North Carolina. The company is operated stirctly by family, so each restaurant is filled with us! For over 70 years we have headquartered in Durham, Nc. Being immigrants it was easy to get home sick and miss our families authentic meals. So we teamed up to serve our lovely authentic culture right to you in the United States.
